Official indexes are a black box and they only go back so far. You remember what things cost — a movie in 1985, rent in 1972, a candy bar when you were a kid — and some of you have the receipts, menus, and ads to prove it. Every submission is checked for plausibility, reviewed, and folded into the open dataset with credit to the source. This is the data no one else has.
